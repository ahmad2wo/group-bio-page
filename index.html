🚀 Our Mission

We are Team Bomba — Ahmad Idris and Mohamed Shire, senior Computer Science students at UNC Charlotte. Our mission is to write clean, working code that solves real problems. We believe that great software comes from teamwork, communication, and never giving up when things get hard. As we finish our senior year, we are focused on growing as engineers and being ready for the industry.

📋 Team Rules

  • Communicate early — don't go silent when you're stuck
  • Respect each other's time and schedule
  • Commit your code often so nothing gets lost
  • Ask for help before you waste hours stuck on one problem
  • Celebrate the small wins, not just the big ones
  • Be honest — if something isn't working, say so
  • Show up ready to contribute every time
